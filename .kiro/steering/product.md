# Product Overview

**Kosmix Spaces** is a premium workspace marketplace platform that connects businesses with high-quality workspace solutions.

## Core Features

- **Workspace Discovery**: Browse and search premium workspace listings with advanced filtering
- **Partner Portal**: Workspace providers can register, submit listings, and manage their properties
- **Admin Dashboard**: Comprehensive management interface for listings, partners, leads, and analytics
- **Lead Management**: Track and manage workspace inquiries and visit requests
- **Analytics**: Performance monitoring and business intelligence
- **Multi-role Authentication**: Separate authentication flows for partners and administrators

## User Roles

- **Public Users**: Browse and search workspaces, submit inquiries
- **Partners**: Workspace providers who can submit and manage listings (requires admin approval)
- **Administrators**: Platform managers with full access to all features and data

## Business Model

Premium workspace marketplace focusing on quality over quantity, with partner approval workflows and comprehensive lead tracking.
